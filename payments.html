Enterprise Payments Management System

We restructured payments by centralising financial workflows into a single system, reducing finance-related support tickets by ~25–35% and cutting manual reconciliation effort by ~30%.

Problem

As Xoxoday Plum scaled with large enterprise clients, finance teams began actively using the platform to manage high-volume transactions and budgets.

However, payment data was scattered across the product, with redemption and financial data mixed together, making it difficult to isolate and manage financial information. This led to -

  • Delays in financial closing
  • Increased support dependencies
  • Reduced trust in financial transparency
  • Risk of accounting mistakes

Before: Redemption and financial data combined in a single Reports tab

There was a clear need to create a structured payment experience that gives finance teams clear visibility, audit-ready data, and independent control, without relying on support or engineering.

Role

I led the experience design end to end, defining the problem, restructuring the IA, and partnering with the PM and stakeholders to drive key product decisions.

Strategy and Direction

We focused on understanding how finance teams were actually managing payments.

  • Payment information was scattered, forcing teams to jump between sections
  • Ledger and redemption data were mixed, making reconciliation slow and unclear
  • Teams relied on manual downloads and offline reconciliation, increasing effort and error risk

Finance teams lacked a reliable, single source of truth. Our direction was to build a dedicated payments system, a single space for all financial operations.

Solution

Clean Report Separation

  • Redemption reports now show only redemption data
  • Payment data moved entirely to the Payments section

This removed data overlap, improved reporting clarity, and reduced accounting errors caused by mixed financial and redemption information.

Financial workflows moved to Payments while Reports focus only on redemption data

Wallet Overview

  • Current balance visibility
  • Recent credit and debit activity
  • Quick view of outstanding invoices

This centralised financial visibility into one clear space, allowing finance teams to instantly understand fund status without navigating across multiple sections.

Wallet overview with balance, pending funds, and recent activity

Transaction History (Ledger-Only View)

  • Clean credit and debit breakdown
  • Filterable transaction logs
  • No redemption noise

By separating ledger data from redemption activity, reconciliation became faster, clearer, and less prone to confusion.

Invoice Management

  • View open and paid invoices
  • Make payments directly
  • Download invoice records

Bringing invoice tracking and payments inside the platform reduced manual coordination and allowed finance teams to complete payment workflows independently.

Impact

  • Reduced finance-related support tickets by ~25–35%
  • Cut manual reconciliation effort by ~30%
  • Reduced operational confusion across finance workflows
  • Enabled finance teams to independently manage payments and invoices
  • Improved clarity and control over financial data across the platform

Summary

This project transformed a fragmented payment experience into a structured financial system. By separating financial workflows from reward operations, we improved clarity, reduced risk, and strengthened enterprise trust.

It marked a shift from a reward-focused tool to an enterprise-ready platform.
